LINK COUNT

Understanding traffic volumes and the composition of various vehicle classes is vital for effective strategic planning, infrastructure development, and ongoing maintenance. Our comprehensive surveys deliver valuable insights into Average Daily Traffic (ADT) and Peak Hour Traffic (PHT), along with their detailed compositions. This data enables informed decision-making.

QUEUE LENGTH ANALYSIS

We provide customized queue data solutions designed to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our services include detailed metrics such as maximum and minimum queue lengths during any specified time interval, average queue lengths for comprehensive analysis, and queue sizes observed at the start of the green phase. Additionally, we conduct queue delay surveys to offer insights into traffic flow efficiency and potential bottlenecks. These tailored datasets empower our clients to make informed decisions for traffic management and infrastructure planning.

SERVICING

We possess extensive expertise in monitoring and analyzing servicing activities on all types of roads. Our services encompass tracking the entry and exit times of delivery vehicles, observing their parking patterns, and recording their delivery destinations. Additionally, we gather and document other relevant activities that occur during the servicing process. The collected information is meticulously organized and supplemented with vehicle classification data, ensuring a detailed and comprehensive overview.

TRAIN STATION SURVEYS

We carry out comprehensive surveys at train stations, collecting a diverse array of data to gain insights into station activities. This includes tracking boarding and alighting patterns for trains, as well as monitoring pedestrian movement on platforms and in subway areas. We also gather information on drop-off and pick-up activities at station entrances, providing a detailed understanding of passenger flow. Additionally, we measure waiting times for taxis and private vehicles, ensuring that all aspects of station transportation are thoroughly captured. Our surveys offer valuable data that helps optimize station operations and improve overall efficiency.

ANPR SURVEYS

We are capable of validating the number plates captured by Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) systems and cross-referencing them across multiple locations. By leveraging this data, we can accurately track vehicle movements and ensure consistency in the identification process. Depending on the specific needs of our clients, we compile and deliver a detailed Origin-Destination Matrix. This matrix provides a clear overview of vehicle travel patterns, offering valuable insights into traffic flow and helping clients make informed decisions based on precise, location-based data.

BUS STATION SURVEYS

By utilizing video footage, we carefully monitor critical bus operations to ensure accurate data collection. This includes tracking precise arrival and departure times, as well as recording boarding and alighting counts, which are further segmented by gender and age for detailed analysis. We also capture information on bus service numbers, providing a clear picture of each route’s activity. Additionally, we tailor our data collection to meet the specific requirements of our clients, ensuring that any additional relevant information is gathered to enhance the overall analysis and support informed decision-making.

TAXI RANK

We have extensive experience in monitoring taxi ranks, allowing us to gather a comprehensive set of data on taxi operations. Our services include tracking taxi arrival and departure times, as well as their position in the queue at any given moment. We also record whether a taxi departs the rank with a passenger or not, ensuring accurate reporting on passenger activity. In addition, we identify and document any unusual behaviors or irregularities, providing a thorough overview of taxi rank operations. This data helps optimize management and improve the overall efficiency of taxi services.
